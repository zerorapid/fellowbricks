<p>I need to stop you from making the most common, exhausting mistake in the modern job search. You’ve probably been told that looking for a job is a "numbers game." You know the advice: "Just send out 100 resumes a week, and eventually, something will stick!"</p>

<p>Let me save you weeks of soul-crushing burnout: applying to 100+ jobs with a standard resume is a complete waste of your time.</p>

<p>Here is the reality of how hiring works right now. When you mass-apply, you are treating your job search like spam. You aren't customizing your application, you aren't researching the company, and you are relying on a lifeless PDF to do all the talking. Because recruiters receive hundreds of these generic applications, your resume gets lost in the black hole of the Applicant Tracking System. You feel "productive" because you clicked submit, but your callback rate stays at zero.</p>

<p>You and I both know that quality always beats quantity. Instead of applying to 100 jobs with a basic resume, what if you applied to just 10 highly targeted jobs, but included a customized portfolio link with each one?</p>

<p>When you shift your strategy from mass-applying to high-value targeting, your success rate skyrockets. A tailored portfolio shows the hiring manager that you actually understand their company's problems and that you have the specific skills to solve them. Stop burning yourself out playing the numbers game. Invest that energy into building a portfolio that makes every single application count.</p>
