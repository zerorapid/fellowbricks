Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting your portfolio.

We offer 2 minor revisions for copy, color tweaks, or small layout adjustments after delivery. However, complete structural revamps or redesigns are not included in the package. If you'd like a major overhaul later, we can discuss custom pricing for a redesign project.

For the Framer Premium package, there are absolutely zero maintenance charges for the first year. For the Self-Hosted package, basic updates are included at no extra cost. We believe in transparent pricing with no surprises.

After the first year, standard domain and hosting renewal rates apply. Domain renewals typically cost ₹800-₹1,200/year, and hosting renewals range from ₹2,000-₹4,000/year depending on the plan. We'll notify you 30 days before renewal with clear pricing.

Once we receive all your content (resume, project details, photos), your portfolio is typically delivered within 48-72 hours. For the Framer Premium package, delivery is usually within 24-48 hours as we prioritize premium clients.

Our standard package includes a single-page portfolio. Additional pages or sections can be added later at a nominal fee. The blog feature is already included, so you can publish unlimited articles without additional page costs.

While we primarily target engineering and tech students in AP & Telangana, our portfolios work for any final-year student or recent graduate—from MBA to BBA to Design. Anyone who wants a professional online presence is welcome!
