Refund Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025

Contact: hello@fellowbricks.site

Our refund policy is simple and transparent, as outlined in our Terms of Service:

  • Before we start work: Full refund (minus payment gateway charges, if any).
  • After work starts: 30% refund if just started; 20% if design is in progress; 10% if development is in progress.
  • After delivery: No refund. You must use your 2 included revisions if you want changes.
  • No refund if: You don't respond for 14+ days (project marked abandoned), you misbehave with our team, or third-party services have already been paid.
  • Approved refunds take 10–15 working days to process to your original payment method.
  • If we cancel without reason: A pro-rata refund will be given for work not done.

Please contact hello@fellowbricks.site for any refund requests.
